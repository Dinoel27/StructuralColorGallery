MEGUMI NAGAINIGHT TREES

To complement the artwork of Megumi Nagai, Structural Color Gallery's space in Brooklyn has been transformed into a nocturnal forest, creating a captivating atmosphere. The use of live trees adds a sense of authenticity and connection to the environment, allowing visitors to feel as though they are truly immersed in a forest. The goal is to not only see the artwork but also to step into the artist's mind and perspective.
